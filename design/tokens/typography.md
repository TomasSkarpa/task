## Typography tokens

IBM Plex Sans for UI and tasks. IBM Plex Mono for dates and Jira keys.

`--font-scale: 1.075` on `html`.

| Role | Size | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| `heading-xl` | 2rem | 600 | Page title (Today) |
| `heading-m` | 1.125rem | 600 | Modal title |
| `body` | 1rem | 400 | Task text |
| `body-small` | 0.875rem | 400 | Helper copy |
| `label` | 0.875rem | 500 | Buttons |
| `meta` | 0.8125rem | 400 mono | Date, Jira key, carryover |
