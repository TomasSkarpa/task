# Spark prompts

Abstract, person-agnostic nudges. Sync to `src/lib/data/spark-prompts.ts`.

- Verb-first, actionable line
- Use `**emphasis**` for the heart of the prompt
- No names, no ticket keys

## Prompts

Thank **someone who helped you** today with one specific sentence

Give **a teammate** a compliment they have actually earned

Recognize **someone's craft** in work they shipped recently

Tell **whoever deserves it** you noticed their **care in the details**

Message **someone** about an **idea of theirs** that made your day easier

Offer **a teammate** help on something small, without being asked

Share **specific praise** with someone whose work is easy to overlook

Thank **whoever unblocked you** and name what they did

Send **a note of appreciation** to someone who showed patience under pressure

Acknowledge **someone's effort** on something that did not make the spotlight

Tell **a colleague** you value how they **show up for the team**

Reach out to **someone you respect** and say what you respect about their work

Give **honest thanks** to whoever made your last sprint less painful

Recognize **quiet consistency** in someone who delivers without fanfare

Offer **encouragement** to someone taking on something new

Celebrate **a small win** with someone who earned it
